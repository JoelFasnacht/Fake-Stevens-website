Payment Schedule for Academic Year 2021-2022

Students/Parents are now able to make Tuition, Room and/or Meal Plan Payments Only online using Visa, MasterCard or Discover Card at the following link:

https://stevenscollege.edu/students-pay-online/

Total Semester Charges minus Total Semester Awarded Financial Aid = Total Semester Cost

Fall Semester 2021

First Payment = 1/3 of Total Fall Semester Cost – Due August 2, 2021

Second Payment = 1/3 of Total Fall Semester Cost – Due September 24, 2021

Third Payment = 1/3 of Total Fall Semester Cost – Due October 29, 2021

Spring Semester 2022

First Payment = 1/3 of Total Spring Semester Cost – Due January 10, 2022

Second Payment = 1/3 of Total Spring Semester Cost – Due February 25, 2022

Third Payment = 1/3 of Total Spring Semester Cost – Due March 28, 2022
